Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

BuzzNews is a news aggregation service. We collect minimal data required to deliver our service.

What We Collect

We do not require account creation or collect personal information. We may collect:

  • Anonymous usage metrics (pages viewed, time of visit) to improve our service.
  • Your language preference (English or Hindi) stored in a browser cookie.

Third-Party Services

Content on BuzzNews is aggregated from publicly available RSS feeds and APIs including Hacker News, Reddit, GDELT, and Tavily Search. We do not control the data practices of these third parties.

Cookies

We use a single functional cookie to remember your language preference. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

Data Retention

Aggregated articles are retained for up to one year. Raw source data is retained for up to 30 days.

Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at privacy@buzznews.in
